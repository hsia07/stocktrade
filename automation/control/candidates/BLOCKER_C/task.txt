=== PROBLEM STATEMENT ===

After BLOCKER_H (Telegram handler) and BLOCKER_I (dispatch scaffold), the
system can dispatch next-round scaffolds, but there is no mechanism to
auto-inject round-specific mandatory sections into the scaffold. Each
blocker round has its own unique requirements that must be documented in
the candidate scaffold's task.txt and evidence.json at creation time, not
manually added later.

=== SOLUTION ===

- ROUND_INJECTION_MAP: Dict[str, Dict] mapping round_id to mandatory_sections
- Three active rounds: BLOCKER_C, BLOCKER_D, AUTO_ADVANCE_OPERATIONAL
- Two roadmap-only rounds: AUTO_ADVANCE_V0_2, AUTO_ADVANCE_V1_0 (never injected)
- inject_into_scaffold(): enriches task.txt with round-specific requirements,
  enriches evidence.json with round-scoped metadata
- Never injects roadmap_only sections (v0.2/v1.0 are blocked)
- Never injects sections from other rounds (no cross-round contamination)
- verify_injection(): machine-checkable scope, roadmap, contamination checks
- verify_no_cross_round_contamination(): exhaustive check against all rounds
